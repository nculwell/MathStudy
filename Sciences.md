  - [5: Statistical Physics Part 1, 3e](https://smile.amazon.com/dp/0750633727)
  - [6: Fluid Mechanics, 2e](https://smile.amazon.com/dp/0750627670)
  - [7: Theory of Elasticity, 3e](https://smile.amazon.com/dp/075062633X)
